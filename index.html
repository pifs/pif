ABOUT US

Playin' It Forward Sports, Inc. (PIFS), a non-profit, 501c3 organization, was founded to provide quality, competitive sports to youth, as well as teach them life lessons through sport participation, and through the use of our proprietary leadership and character development curriculum.

Each coach, director and official is trained and dedicated to the principles that guide PIFS. It is expected that all participants, parents, and guardians that become members of PIFS sports and organizations will abide by the rules of fair play and respect.

Every season, PIFS hosts tournaments, fundraisers and events that support local and national charities. These events provide young athletes with an opportunity to be a "Person for Others" and truly give back to their community in a meaningful way. This season, PIFS and the Blazers will host events that will specifically address the needs of the following organizations: Peace Players International, St. Baldrick's Foundation, and the Durham Rescue Mission. We are excited about helping these great organizations and we hope that you will be a part of that experience. Please feel free to inquire about volunteering your time and expertise to make our events better. We thank you and look forward to having your son play for us!
